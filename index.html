Building things people use.

Currently building Tally, an AI compliance and security platform that answers questions before the regulators ask them. Previously cofounder of Seis (YC W22), product leader at OC Tanner, and curriculum and mobile training at DevMountain.

Background

I studied economics, which shaped how I think about products — incentives, trade-offs, where value comes from. I learned to code because I had too many ideas and got tired of waiting for someone else to build them.

Shipped dozens of apps along the way — some for clients, some for fun. A sunrise calendar for a friend. A letter sounds app to teach my daughter to read.

Now

Thinking about small teams, craft, and the relationship between product and design. Interested in how AI changes what it means to build software.

When I'm not working I'm with Ashley and my kids. I play lacrosse, golf poorly, and occasionally convince myself to train for something ambitious.
